What is PKCS Digital Certificate?

Certificate is a document that binds identity such as IP addresses,
email address, phone number, real life credentials with a public
key of any kind(DFKX, Digital Signatures).

It can be used in cryptography processes to establish a shared secret
through DFKX or it can be used to bind an identity to a document(files/softwares)
with a public key of any kind and it can be used to identify if an individual
within a company/corporate/business have access to the system.
